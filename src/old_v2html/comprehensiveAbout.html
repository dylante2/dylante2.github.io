  • May 2019 Jane Street SEE In early March, a friend of mine told me to apply to this program Jane Street was hosting called SEE (Spring Educational Event). I applied, though skeptical that I had what it took for the company.

    Lo and behold, I was able to move through the interview stages and got accepted. Out of 2000+ applicants, me and 31 other students had the opportunity to travel to NYC (all expenses paid) and learn about various market basics, probability concepts, and most importantly, arbitrage.

    Meeting everyone else was enough to make the trip worth it. But most importantly, I left the trip feeling a much higher self-worth. I realized that I was much more capable than I thought. Personally, I think the fact that I went to Pitt and not some top 10 powerhouse school really elicited a lot of self doubt, but my time at Jane Street really helped see through this and I'm forever grateful
